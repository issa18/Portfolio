A Family Business

State Code Electric started with a dream. Back in 2010 after working many years in the industrial electricity business Antonio, owner and CEO of State Code Electric, decided to take a chance and open up his own electrical business. Only having his little red car, tools, and knowledge he set off to find customers and put his name out there. With his families help and support his business started growing. After 10+ years State Code Electric is now a well known electrical company around the Houston area.
